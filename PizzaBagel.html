Pizza Bagels

You can't be pour enough for this to be better than Little Caesar's

Ingredients

  • Bagels
  • Mozzarella, shredded
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Any other toppings you can afford

Instructions

  1. Toast the Bagels
  2. Put Sauce on top of the Bagels
  3. Put the cheese on top of the sauce
  4. Put the toppings on top
  5. Microwave if for 30 seconds
  6. Check the Pizza Bagels, if it doesn't look good, put it in for 15 more seconds
  7. Repeat until at appropriate temperature
  8. Throw it away and go to Little Caesar's. It's only $5... what's wrong with you?
